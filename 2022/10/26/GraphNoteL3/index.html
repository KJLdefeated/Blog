Kruskal’s Algorithm

  • Just sort all weighted edges and pick the one with least weight until the graph connected and without any cycle.

Prim’s Algorithm

  • Randomly pick a root. Add the least weight edge that incident to root. Then find the least weight edge among all edge that incident to the endpoint of TTT until TTT cotain all vertex and connected.
